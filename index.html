Features

Let's be honest, YouTube is great, but it's not designed for music. Headset brings the comfort of a native music player on top of YouTube's platform. The result is a slick experience and added functionality you won't find on youtube.com

A Library of Millions.

YouTube has millions of songs, from massive hits to rare gems to cult classics, with more added every minute. It’s more music than you could listen to in a lifetime.

Music Discovery Done Right.

Find what to listen to next by following a music subreddits, with over 80 different stations, including /r/listenToThis, /r/chillout, /r/electronic and many more. Explore curated playlists, updated daily, and hand-picked by the reddit community.

Make it Yours.

Build a collection of your favorites, so they’re always close at hand. Save playlists and channels directly from YouTube, keep a "listen later" list of songs, or even capture hours of free MIT courses and TED talks. The possibilities are endless.

That's Not All

Cross Platform

Headset is available for Windows, Linux (Debian, Redhat) and MacOs. You can even build it from source on a custom environment.

Themes

Choose between Dark and Light themes. Custom themes to come soon!

Last.fm

One click integration with the popular social music service. Scrobble all your songs and have the option to edit the information.

Private and Secure

All data, credentials, and cookies are transmitted over a secure SSL connection, to keep you invisible over the network.

Open Source

To ensure security and stability, a big part of Headset's source is kept open.

Cloud Syncing

Using multiple computers? No worries. Simply log in and get back to your music.
