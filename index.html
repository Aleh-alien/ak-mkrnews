Headset is a desktop app that turns YouTube into a world class music streaming service. Listen to everything YouTube has to offer on a beautiful user interface that's built for productivity. Create collections, tune-in to a music sub-reddit or quickly play that song you’ve had stuck in your head all day!

Find. Everything.

With Headset, it takes 3 seconds to find what you want. Search through millions of songs, albums and playlists. Once you find your track, Headset will automatically pick similar songs and add them to the queue for non-stop all day sessions.

Radio, by Reddit.

Enjoy the best of both worlds with over 80 different radio stations, powered by the best and most active music sub-reddits. Including /r/listenToThis, /r/90smusic, /r/electronic and many more. Get fresh picks every single day! (sometimes minutes.)

Make it Your Own.

Collections are a great way to mix up favorite tunes, save playlists directly from YouTube, keep a "listen later" list of songs, or even capture hours of MIT courses and TED talks. The possibilities are endless.
