“Non-Personally-Identifying Information” is information that, without the aid of additional information, cannot be directly associated with a specific person. “Personally-Identifying Information,” by contrast, is information such as a name or email address that, without more, can be directly associated with a specific person. Company analyzes Non-Personally-Identifying Information gathered from users of the Software to help Company better understand how the Software is being used.
